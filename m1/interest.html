My General Interests

Aside from programming and computer science, I have a few other interests that I enjoy spending my time on.

Interest in the Arts

Practicing the Art

One of my main interests is drawing. I like to draw my own characters with a potential goal to one day make a comic in the future. I have also done a little bit of animation as I took a course in High School. It really helped getting a flow of imagining characters in movement.

Consuming the Art

Continuing the topic of art, I also enjoy reading manga and watch anime. Some manga I have read were Tokyo Ghoul, Magi: The Labyrinth of Magic, and Vagabond. Almost every one of these have had an anime adaptation to which I have also watched. Manga and anime has been a huge inspiration in my art style and creativity.

Aside from reading manga and watching anime, I enjoy listening to music. I enjoy listening to a wide range of genres, including jazz, pop, classical, electronic music, and more. I love how music has the power to evoke emotions and create a sense of atmosphere, which is great for coming up with cool art ideas/ composition. I really enjoy listening to scores from video games or tv shows as they really set the tone for a scene and vibe that the audience should be having.
